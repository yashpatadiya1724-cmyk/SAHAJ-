Accessibility Status System

🟢

Fully Accessible

Score 7–10/10. Has ramps, elevators, accessible toilets, and wide doors. Suitable for wheelchair users.

🟡

Partially Accessible

Score 4–6/10. Some accessibility features present but not complete. May require assistance.

🔴

Not Accessible

Score 0–3/10. Significant barriers exist. Needs urgent accessibility improvements.
